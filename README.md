
# AI Article Retriever

A tool that automatically retrieves and filters AI-related articles from emails received from various news sources, including the New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Medium, and others. Stay on top of the latest AI news without the noise â€” only the categories that matter to you land in one place.

## Features

- Connects to your email inbox and scans incoming newsletters
- Filters articles by AI category (e.g., machine learning, generative AI, robotics, AI policy)
- Supports multiple news sources: New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Medium, and more
- Outputs a clean, organized list of relevant articles
